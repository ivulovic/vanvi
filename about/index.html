About

The content you see on this site has a special value for me.

Initially, there was no need for a site, but as time went on, a lot of created music content was collected and I had a feeling that it was too worthless if it just stood on a computer disk and was not visible to anyone.

I like to play music, so this site is just one of my hobbies that I wanted to emphasize and make it part of my portfolio.

Everything I have created so far, as well as what I have yet to do, will be published here.

In case you wanted to connect or talk to me, you can find me on Instagram or Twitter. I've already done few collaborations with friends, so if you wanted to work with me - say Hi!

Thank you for visiting.
